The typical AQHI is calculated as the median hourly AQHI for the current hour of the day and current week of the year, based on data from your selected location.
This calculation uses a 10-year period from 2014 to 2023, where at least 5 years of data are available for that location.

These values were generated using the NAPS Data Analysis Toolbox, which sources raw data from the National Air Pollution Surveillance (NAPS) program, part of Environment and Climate Change Canada.
