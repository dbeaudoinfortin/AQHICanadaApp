O3 refers to ground-level ozone, a gas that forms when nitrogen oxides (NOx) and volatile organic compounds (VOCs) pollutants react with sunlight. Ozone is a harmful air pollutant and high levels are linked to respiratory issues and other health problems. Ozone is measured in parts per billion (ppb).

This ozone scale is based on guidelines from the 2025 Canadian Ambient Air Quality Standards (CAAQS) 8-hour management levels for outdoor air across Canada. These levels are established by the Canadian Council of Ministers of the Environment (CCME).

The purpose of this scale is to provide context to the raw ozone values. It should not be interpreted as a direct representation of human health risk.
