SO2 refers to sulphur dioxide, a gas that mainly comes from burning sulphur-containing fuels in industrial processes. Sulphur dioxide is a harmful air pollutant and high levels are linked to respiratory issues and other health problems. Sulphur dioxide is measured in parts per billion (ppb).

This sulphur dioxide scale is guidelines from based on the 2025 Canadian Ambient Air Quality Standards (CAAQS) 1-hour management levels for outdoor air across Canada. These levels are established by the Canadian Council of Ministers of the Environment (CCME).

The purpose of this scale is to provide context to the raw sulphur dioxide values. It should not be interpreted as a direct representation of human health risk.
