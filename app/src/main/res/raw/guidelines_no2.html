NO2 refers to nitrogen dioxide, a nitrogen oxides (NOx) family gas that mainly comes from high-temperature combustion sources like traffic and industry. Nitrogen dioxide is a harmful air pollutant and high levels are linked to respiratory issues and other health problems. Nitrogen dioxide is measured in parts per billion (ppb).

This nitrogen dioxide scale is based on guidelines from the 2025 Canadian Ambient Air Quality Standards (CAAQS) 1-hour management levels for outdoor air across Canada. These levels are established by the Canadian Council of Ministers of the Environment (CCME).

The purpose of this scale is to provide context to the raw nitrogen dioxide values. It should not be interpreted as a direct representation of human health risk.
